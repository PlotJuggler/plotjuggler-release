The tracker

The "tracker" is a vertical line that has two goals:

  1. To display the value of the time-series at a certain instant of time.
  2. To trigger the plugins in the category "StatePublisher".

The tracker can be enabled and disabled using the button "A" (bottom left or the central area).

It can be moved either:

  • moving the slidebar at the bottom ("B") or
  • pressing SHIFT+Left-Mouse on the a specific position of the plot.
